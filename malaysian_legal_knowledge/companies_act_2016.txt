PART I - PRELIMINARY

Section 2 - Interpretation
In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires:
"company" means a company incorporated under this Act or under any corresponding previous written law;
"director" includes any person occupying the position of director by whatever name called;

PART II - INCORPORATION

Section 14 - Requirements for incorporation
(1) One or more persons may form a company by subscribing their names to a constitution and complying with the requirements of this Act.
